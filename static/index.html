Power chart legend

Colors and markers follow battery power in watts (positive into the pack, negative out).

Charging
Segments where battery power is above zero: the system is drawing power into the battery from the adapter.
Discharging
Segments where battery power is below zero: the battery is feeding the system.
Adapter
Total power delivered by the adapter (voltage × current). The difference between adapter and battery charging power is consumed by the system.
Mode switch
Dashed vertical lines mark times when power crossed zero between two samples.

Temperature chart

The shaded bands use a 38 °C horizontal guide.

Comfort
Readings below 38 °C are shown in the cooler-toned region.
Elevated
At or above 38 °C, the chart uses warmer colors.
Dashed vertical lines
When the trace crosses 38 °C between two samples, a vertical marker shows approximately when that crossing happened.

Battery chart legend

Colors use macOS charging flags from each stored sample.

Charging
The battery is actively accepting charge while on AC or USB power.
On battery
Not on external power: the Mac is running from the internal battery.
External power
Plugged in, but not in an active charging state.
Mode switch
Dashed vertical lines show category changes between consecutive samples.
